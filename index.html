Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is the Vibe Score?
A decision signal (0-100) weighted by business impact. It's not a vanity metric — it reflects clarity, trust signals, and UX psychology that directly affect conversion.
Are there file size and language limits?
Starter/Pro: Up to 250KB file uploads, English-only PDF reports. Agency: Up to 25MB file uploads, reports in 5 languages (EN, IT, ES, FR, DE).
How does VibeBot work?
VibeBot has full context of your audit. Ask "What should I fix first?" or "Why is my conversion score low?" and get instant, contextual strategy. Starter: 15 msg/mo, Pro: 50 msg/mo, Agency: Unlimited.
How do Notion/Trello integrations work?
One-click export. Your prioritized findings become task cards automatically, ready for your team to execute. Available on all plans.
Is it safe?
Enterprise-grade encryption. Stripe handles all payments securely. Your data is processed and not stored beyond what's necessary for the audit.
Is VibeAudit just a PDF report?
No. VibeAudit is a live audit platform with an intelligent dashboard. PDF reports are optional exports — the main experience happens in real-time.
Is VibeAudit only for agencies?
No. Agencies use advanced features like white-label and team collaboration, but VibeAudit is designed for CRO consultants, in-house product teams, and founders as well.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a one-time Free Preview so you can experience the audit output and workflow before choosing a plan. Run one audit, see your Vibe Score, then decide.
