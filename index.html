FAQ

Everything you need to know

What exactly is the Vibe Score?
A decision signal (0–100) weighted by business impact. It's not a vanity metric — it reflects clarity, trust signals, and UX psychology that affect conversion on the URL you audited.
What can I audit today?
VibeAudit is focused on live, publicly reachable website URLs. Paste a URL; we analyze what visitors see. Plan limits (audits per month, feature depth) apply per your subscription.
How does VibeBot work?
VibeBot has full context of your audit. Ask "What should I fix first?" or "Why is my score low?" and get contextual guidance. Starter: 15 msg/mo, Pro: 50 msg/mo, Agency: Unlimited.
How do Trello exports work?
Export prioritized findings to Trello so your team can execute — available where the product supports it on your plan.
Is it safe?
Traffic is encrypted in transit. Stripe handles payments. Audit inputs are processed to generate your report; retention follows our Privacy Policy.
Is VibeAudit just a PDF?
No. You get a live dashboard with scores, issues, and history. PDFs are client-ready exports — ideal for proposals and handoffs.
Is VibeAudit only for agencies?
No. Agencies use white-label and team features, but freelancers and consultants use VibeAudit daily for fast, revenue-framed audits on live sites.
Is there a free preview?
We offer a one-time free preview so you can see audit output before choosing a plan. Run one audit on a live URL, review your Vibe Score, then upgrade if it fits.
How accurate are the findings?
VibeAudit combines deterministic signals like PageSpeed, structural checks, screenshot analysis, and site-type-specific UX/CRO heuristics. Findings are designed as a strong expert-grade starting point, not a substitute for a full manual conversion audit. You can review and refine the final report before sharing it.
Can I customize the report before sending it to a client?
Yes. You can review findings, adjust priorities, and add your own notes before delivery. On Agency, you can also apply your own branding so the PDF feels like it came from your team.
